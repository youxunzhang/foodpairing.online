How does food pairing work?

We group ingredients based on complementary flavors, textures, and nutrition. Start with a main ingredient, then layer supporting elements that add freshness, richness, or crunch. Our pairing pages—for example tomato, cheese, and chicken—offer ready-to-use combinations.

Is the food roulette free?

Absolutely. Every roulette tool on FoodPairing.online is free to use. Spin as many times as you like across the all-meal wheel, breakfast, lunch, and dinner pages.

Can I use the meal randomizer for every meal?

Yes! Each meal-specific wheel uses ingredients tailored to that time of day. Spin the breakfast roulette for energizing starts, grab lunch ideas on the midday wheel, and finish with the dinner roulette.

How do I customize spins for dietary needs?

Use the results as a template. Swap proteins, grains, or sauces to match your dietary preferences. Our pairing pages highlight vegetarian, gluten-free, and high-protein options you can mix in.

Can I share roulette results with friends?

Yes. Save your favorite spins or meal pairings and send the link. Planning a group meal? Suggest a few options generated from the roulette and vote together.
